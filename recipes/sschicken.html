Sweet and Sour Chicken

Description

Crispy fried chicken pieces with pineapple and bell pepper

Served with any tangy and sweet sauce

Ingredients

    For the sauce:
  • Pineapple, White sugar, White Vinegar, Orange food coloring.
    • For the batter:
  • Cornstarch, Self-rising flour, Vegetable oil, 1 Egg, Salt, White pepper.
    • Chicken
  • Cut eight boneless, skinless chicken breast halfes into 1-inch cubes for this sweet and sour recipe.
    • Pineapple and green pepper:
  • Pineapple chunks and two green bell peppers (cut into 1-inch pieces) give the dish color and flavor.

Steps

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water with pineapple juice, sugar, vinegar, and orange food coloring.
  2. Make a cornstarch slurry, then mix it into the sauce until thickened.
  3. Make the batter, then stir in the chicken pieces until well coated.
  4. Fry the chicken.
  5. Layer the peppers, pineapple, and chicken on a platter.
  6. Pour the sauce over the top.
