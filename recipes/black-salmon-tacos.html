Blackened Salmon Tacos

Recipe Description

A quick and healthy dish

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe mangos -peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 avacado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 1 orange or red bell pepper - Diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder, or to your taste
  • 1 pinch Salt
  • 1 tablespoon sweet and spicy seafood rub
  • Salt and ground black pepper to your taste
  • 2 skin-on salmon fillets
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil, or as needed, divided
  • 6 Corn Tortillas
  • Large limes, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Combine mangos, avocado, orange bell pepper, and jalapeno in a large bowl to make salsa.

    Add lime juice, cilantro, and salt; mix to combine. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate.
